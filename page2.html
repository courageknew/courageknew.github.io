Project 1

Social Clock: Social Trajectory under 12,275 Data

The work presents the social clock change from the perspective of multi-dimensional data, and scrutinizes the crux of the times in the development of Chinese society since the reform and opening up.

click here to check our website
Data Collection

We crawled authoritative statistical data from the National Statistical Yearbook, China Population Statistical Yearbook, China Labor Statistical Yearbook, the Seventh National Population Census, the Blue Book of Education, and the Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China, and manually clustered and summarized the data over the years.

Data analysis

We attempted to analyze the data through time and correlation to construct a deep narrative in the changing social clock in conjunction with the current state of social development.The narrative is organized into four chapters: “Education,” “Employment,” “Marriage and Childbearing,” “Decline in Elderly Life,” and “The Reverse Social Clock.

Data Visualization

With the help of code, we visualized narratives such as “region and years of schooling”, “age and unemployment”, “gender and age at first marriage”, “my health and main sources of livelihood”, etc. Visualization of narratives such as “region and years of schooling”, “age and unemployment rate”, “gender and age at first marriage”, “health status and main sources of livelihood”, etc.

Web Design

We used html, css, javascript, Vue.js, and Bootstrap frameworks to build the web pages, and used dynamic UI-WEB features such as open-screen depth-of-field parallax scrolling, AI painting application, hover icon color change zoom effects, mobile icon display, rotating images of five major panels, and file-by-file display.
